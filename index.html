Football Scores Counter App

Making it easy to record and save play scores

WHY I BUILT THE COUNTER APP?

Most times, while playing football in our community. we usually have the problem of scores being argued upon, at almost all time. Both teams arguing that, their scores are being alterated. We most times go home or path ways in sad form, which ruins the joy of our game. To avoid this in further matches, I came up with the counter app that records and saves scorelines using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript. This method of mine, has since then made us play without settling to bad ground at the end of the game.

COUNTER APP

As popularly known, the football match is being played between two teams. The kick-off takes place, after the referee has tossed the coin, deciding the head and the tail. Where, the head starts kick-off. It's this same principle we do use in playing football in my community. The head kicks-off at start of the game. when a goal is being scored, we record it by clicking on goal(for the side that scored) and at the end of the match, we click on save, to keep our scorelines.
